Who I Am

I'm a first-gen college grad with a BS in Computer Science and Japanese, now working as a software engineer for InfoSys.

With my degree, I hope to find somewhere that I can make myself helpful. I get a huge sense of gratification from the knowledge that the work I do contributes to someone else's well-being. I enjoy working with others to create products that promote joy and learning, and I have been lucky enough to spend two summers and many semesters doing that as a coding instructor and tutor in both public and private settings, professionally and as a volunteer. I get particularly excited about video games, space travel, and artificial intelligence.
